Sri Lanka, the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean," offers a rich tapestry of experiences for every kind of traveler. From ancient cities steeped in history to breathtaking natural landscapes, the island nation provides a sensory feast. Explore UNESCO World Heritage Sites, encounter diverse wildlife, unwind on pristine beaches, and immerse yourself in a vibrant culture. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or spiritual enlightenment, Sri Lanka has something truly special to offer.

Here are 10 highlights of things to do in Sri Lanka:

  • Explore Ancient Cities
  • Climb Sigiriya Rock
  • Visit Kandy
  • Relax on Southern Beaches
  • Go on a Wildlife Safari
  • Take a Scenic Train Ride
  • Explore Galle Fort
  • Savor Sri Lankan Cuisine
  • Visit Dambulla Cave Temple
  • Wander Tea Plantations
